How it works

Five steps.
One complete application.

Walk through the process once and come out the other side ready to apply — and actually get a response.

01
👤

Set up your profile

Tell us your name, school, city, and what kind of job you're going for. Takes about 90 seconds.

02
📄

Build or improve your resume

No resume? We'll build one from scratch. Have one? Paste it and we'll score it and fix what's weak.

03
🎯

Analyse the job posting

Paste the job URL or description and get a full breakdown: ATS score, keyword gaps, company research, and your match percentage.

04
📋

Get your documents

Your final resume and a tailored cover letter — both ATS-optimised, ready to copy and send.

05
💬

Prep for the interview

5 likely questions with sample answers, 2 scenario questions, a curveball, and smart questions to ask them.

06
🎉

Full summary

See everything you've built in one clean view — profile, resume, job analysis, documents, and interview prep.
