Questions?

The ones we get asked most.

Is it really free? +
Yes. The free plan gives you the full 5-step process — profile, resume, job analysis, documents, and interview prep — with no credit card required. The only limit on free is 1 job analysis per month.
Do I need to create an account? +
No account, no sign-up, no email needed. Your progress saves automatically in your browser. If you clear your browser data, your progress clears too — so Pro users who want cross-device access will have account options coming soon.
Will Claude make things up about me? +
We specifically built against this. Every claim in your generated resume is checked against what you actually told us — we call it the hallucination check. If something appears in your resume that wasn't in your input, you'll be asked to confirm or remove it before the document is finalised.
Does it work for all types of jobs? +
It's optimised for first and second jobs — retail, food service, grocery, pharmacy, hardware, clothing, and similar customer-facing roles. The AI knows the keywords, formats, and interview styles for all of these. It works for other jobs too but the sweet spot is that first 1-2 years of work experience.
How long does the full process take? +
Most students finish in 20–35 minutes start to finish. If you have no resume at all, expect 30–40 minutes for the first run. After that, analysing a new job posting takes under 5 minutes.
Can I use it on my phone? +
Yes, it's mobile-first. Everything works on your phone — typing your resume, pasting job descriptions, reviewing your documents. You'll probably want a bigger screen for the final review, but it's fully functional on mobile throughout.
